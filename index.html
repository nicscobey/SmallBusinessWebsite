About Me

I'm an educator and trainer who is committed to helping people recognize, communicate, and mobilize their full talents to create a better world. Blending skills in career coaching, along with principles of industrial-organizational psychology, I help individuals and teams maximize their abilities and ROI.

Through my educational and professional experience, I found the value in leveraging technology to make a massive impact on the ways we instruct and train. With a background in education, human services, and technology, my goal is to leverage resources and data to implement best-in-class learning and development programs that will support the success of individuals, teams, and our global community.
